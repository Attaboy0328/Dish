# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People deciding what to cook at home, usually on a phone and often while hungry, busy, or standing in a grocery store. Their primary job is to settle on a varied, practical menu with minimal thought.

## Product Purpose

Open three approachable home-style recipes each day, turn indecision into a short enjoyable ritual, and reward repeat use without making meal planning feel like work.

## Brand Personality

Warm, decisive, playful. The product should feel as calm and dependable as a native iOS utility, with delight concentrated in the daily reveal.

## Anti-references

Avoid dashboard density, beige “AI lifestyle app” styling, casino-like prize effects, emoji-heavy navigation, decorative gradients, oversized rounded cards, and unfamiliar custom controls.

## Design Principles

1. Put the daily decision first; preferences remain within reach but never compete with the reveal.
2. Use familiar mobile controls and predictable navigation so the ritual feels effortless.
3. Reserve animation and color for meaningful state changes, especially the reveal and check-in.
4. Keep every recipe actionable with concise metadata, ingredients, and steps.
5. Make progress motivating without punishing missed days.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA contrast, 44-point touch targets, visible keyboard focus, semantic control states, safe-area support, reduced-motion alternatives, and text that remains legible at mobile zoom levels.
